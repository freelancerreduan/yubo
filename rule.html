What is a reverse transaction?



A reverse trade is a reverse score. For example, if you invest in a game with a score of 0-0, as long as the final result of the two teams is not 0-0, you will win, and the platform will immediately settle the income. (For example, if the score is 1-0, 3-2, 1-3, the player wins)




What is a game?



A match is a straight score. For example, if you put in a 0-0 score in a game, you will only win if both teams end up 0-0. Higher profit, higher risk.
